Frequently Asked Questions

Does VidZip reduce video quality?

VidZip lowers the bitrate while keeping the original resolution. With the Balanced preset, most people can't tell the difference visually, yet the file is ~70% smaller. Choose High Quality for near-lossless, or Space Saver for maximum shrinkage.

Is Pro a subscription?

No. Pro is a one-time purchase of $9.99. No subscription, no recurring charges, no auto-renewal. Buy once, own forever — including all future updates.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. If you previously bought Pro on the same Apple ID, it will be restored instantly at no additional cost.

Where are my videos processed?

Entirely on your iPhone. Videos never upload to any server — there is no server. This keeps your videos private and lets the app work offline.

What video formats are supported?

MP4, MOV, MPEG, and AVI as input. Output is always MP4 (H.264 + AAC) for maximum compatibility across apps and devices.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later.
